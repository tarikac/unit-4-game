Instructions

  • Player receives a random number.
  • Each crystal is assigned a hidden number. The hidden number will be "revealed" after clicking on a crystal.
  • Player clicks on a crystal to add a specific amount of points to the player's total score
  • Player wins if their score matches the random number.
  • Next round will start automatically with a new random number and a set of hidden numbers for the crystals.
  • To reset game (reset wins and losses) please press the reset button.

    • Enjoy!
